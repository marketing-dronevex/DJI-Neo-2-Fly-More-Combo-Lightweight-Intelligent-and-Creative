## Introduction  
The DJI Neo 2 Fly More Combo is DJI’s most compact and portable drone, weighing under 249 grams yet packed with high-end features designed for creators who demand quality and convenience. Whether you’re a vlogger, traveler, or social media influencer, Neo 2 elevates your storytelling with rich 4K HDR video, intelligent flight modes, and an adaptive gimbal that captures cinematic shots effortlessly.

## Compact Design & Extended Flight  
Built for life on the go, the Neo 2 is foldable and lightweight, making it easy to carry anywhere. Its Fly More Combo includes extra batteries and a charging hub, extending flight times up to approximately 38 minutes, so you can capture uninterrupted adventures without constantly swapping batteries.

## Professional-Grade Camera & Imaging  
The drone’s 1/1.3-inch CMOS sensor shoots vivid 4K videos at 60 fps with HDR and supports cinematic slow motion at 120 fps. With a 48MP still camera and dual-format gimbal allowing seamless switching between landscape and vertical modes, this drone is ready for every creative shot from sweeping vistas to social reels.

## Intelligent Flight & Safety Features  
Neo 2 comes equipped with advanced omnidirectional obstacle sensing using lidar and vision sensors for safe, confident flying indoors or outdoors. Gesture and voice controls, combined with smart flight modes such as ActiveTrack and QuickShots, put creative control right at your fingertips.

## Versatile Control Options  
Pilot Neo 2 using the DJI RC-N3 remote with a 10 km transmission range, the intuitive DJI Fly app, or enjoy immersive FPV flying with DJI goggles. Gesture controls and palm landing make it accessible for all skill levels.
